## The Build

- 1978 Toyota Landcruiser
- Netgain Hyper9 HV Electric Motor Kit 
- 30kWh Lithium Batteries
- 37inch water filled Maxxis tyres
- Locked diffs
- Custom suspension lift and components
- Front facing Sonar
- 150m CAN cable running onto dive boat for real time system telemetry and sonar
- Custom designed and fabricated subsea containers for electric power train

## Engineering 

- Electrical system immersed in silicon oil & positive pressure compensated
- Vehicle energy use modelling
- Cusom designed and built pressure chambers for component testing & selection
- Custom control electronics for human undersea interface (in water) into motor controller (in silicon oil)
- Complex shaft coupler interface from motor (in silicon oil) to gearbox
